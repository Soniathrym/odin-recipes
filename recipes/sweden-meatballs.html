Swedish Meatballs

Home

Description

Swedish meatballs, known as svenska kottbullar, are a beloved comfort food featuring tender, seasoned meatballs served in a rich, creamy gravy, often accompanied by egg noodles, mashed potatoes, or rice.

Swedish meatballs origins can be traced back to Ottoman Empire, King Charles XII brought the recipe back to Sweden after spendings years in exile in Ottoman Empire.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup beef stock
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ground black pepper
  • dried rosemary
  • 10 ounces frozen cooked meatballs(thawed)

Directions:

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk together stock,cream,flour,soy sauce, pinch of pepper and rosemary in a large saucepan over low heat until smooth.
  3. Stir occasionally, until thicked, about 10 minutes
  4. Stir in thawed meatballs and contuine cooking until meatballs are heated through.
  5. Taste and adjust seasonings before servings.
