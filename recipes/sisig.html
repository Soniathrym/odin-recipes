Sizzling Sisig

Home

Description:

A Filipino dish originated in Pampanga, the word "sisig" originated from "sisigan" means to make sour.

The original form was a sour snack, something consumed by pregnant women craving something acidic

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound of Pork belly - sliced about half-inch thick
  • salt and pepper(feel free to use other condiments to your liking)
  • 3 pieces of chicken liver
  • 1 big red onion - finely chopped
  • 1-3 calamansi juice
  • 3-5 Red chili peppers
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Season Pork belly slices with salt and pepper, let it marinate overnight or at least 30 mins
  2. Grill Pork belly and chicken liver until well done
  3. Chop Pork belly into fine pieces
  4. Saute Onion and Red chili, add chicken liver and Pork belly
  5. Add salt, pepper and 2 tablespoon soy sauce. stir gently
  6. Add Calamansi Juice, contuine stiring until you feel the hotness/spicyness
  7. Add condiments to your liking
  8. Serve in a sizzling plate and crack an egg on top of it. Alternatively crack the egg while stiring the pan
