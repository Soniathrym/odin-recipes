Pesto Pasta

Description:

Pesto pasta, originated in Genoa, the capital of Liguria region in northwestern Italy.

The word Pesto derives from Italian verb pestare, meaning "to Crush" reffering to the traditional method of preparing method of preparation using a mortar and pestle.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Pesto(Grounded Herbs)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook your onion on olive oil until translucent
  2. Boil your pasta for 8 mins in salted water then drain
  3. Saute Onion and Basil Pesto, season it with salt and pepper and stir afterwards
  4. Add Pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with Cheese.
